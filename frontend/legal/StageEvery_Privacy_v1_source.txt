StageEvery – Privacy Policy (v1.0) Effective date: 27 January 2026

StageEvery respects your privacy.

1.  Information We Collect We may collect the following types of
    personal information:

-   Email address
-   Display name
-   Date of birth (optional)
-   Phone number (optional)
-   Profile images and videos
-   Music-related content and metadata
-   Social media links and external URLs
-   Any other information you voluntarily provide

2.  How We Use Information We use this information to:

-   Create and manage accounts
-   Provide and improve the service
-   Display user profiles as configured by users
-   Communicate important service-related messages

3.  Authentication We use Google OAuth and do not store passwords.

4.  Data Sharing We do not sell personal data. Information is shared
    only with essential service providers (e.g. hosting,
    authentication).

5.  Data Retention Data is retained while your account remains active or
    as required to provide the service. You may request deletion at any
    time.

6.  Security We apply reasonable security measures, but no system is
    completely secure.

7.  Children’s Privacy Not intended for users under 13.

8.  Your Rights You may access, update, or delete your personal data.

9.  Changes Updates to this policy may require renewed consent.

Contact: privacy@stageevery.com
